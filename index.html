Get started

Download one of the wallets from the links above, and you’re all set to go! Alternatively, build your own from the source.

We would suggest staying away from online wallets, as the chance you’ll lose all of your coins is very high!

If you don’t care about sending coins and would just like a receiving address for a paper wallet, you can use our online wallet generator. Once you print the page and/or save your public and private keys, you can give your public key to others to start receiving coins.

Get some coins

The easiest way to obtain coins is to buy them from an exchange. Catcoin is currently listed on:

  • CoinedUp: CAT/BTC, CAT/DOGE
  • CoinEx: CAT/BTC, CAT/LTC
  • Cryptsy: CAT/BTC

If you would like to mine for your own coins, you may consider joining one of the following pools:

  • TeamCatcoin: 0% fee
  • Catpool.pw: 0% fee
  • Coinium: 0% fee
  • Catpool.in: 0% fee
  • NonStopMine: 0% fee
  • Solidpool P2P pool: 1% fee
  • p2pool.name: 1% fee
  • MintPool: 1% fee (0% for donors)
  • Bettercoin: 1% fee
  • Catpool.co: 1% fee
  • Cryptovalley: 1% fee
  • Luckyminers: 1.5% fee

Given your computer’s hashrate, you can see how many coins you will get per day using the NonStopMine mining calculator.
