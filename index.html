Get started

Download one of the wallets from the links above, and you’re all set to go! Alternatively, build your own from the source.

We would suggest staying away from online wallets, as the chance you’ll lose all of your coins is very high!

If you don’t care about sending coins and would just like a receiving address, you can use our paper wallet generator. Once you print the page and/or save your public and private keys, you can give your public key to others to start receiving coins.

ANN: Hard fork (at block 20290)

Catcoin automatically adjusts its mining difficulty every 2016 blocks to ensure that the network finds a new block every ~10 minutes. However, speculative and profit-switching miners caused the last adjustment to way overestimate the network hashrate, and as a result, we are finding blocks every 90 minutes instead. This is making mining, sending, and receiving catcoins unbearably slow. As the next difficulty change won’t be for another four months, this situation, if left unchecked, could irreparably harm the Catcoin ecosystem.

The developer and the community as a whole have sanctioned a modification to Catcoin so that the difficulty will instead adjust every 36 blocks. This will immediately cause a difficulty decrease (making your miners more profitable), but additionally will protect against future profit-switching miners from gaining a huge advantage. In short, this small change has the potential to save Catcoin.

Your current coins will not be affected — in fact, they will likely be worth more after the fork!

If you have any questions or concerns, come to the IRC channel and we’ll be happy to help you out. See you on the flip side!
